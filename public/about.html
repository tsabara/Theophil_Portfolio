About Me

Hello, my name is Theophil Sabara. I was born and raised in a small village called Tumukur, located in Gajapati district, Odisha, India. I completed my schooling at S.D.A High School, Khurda, Odisha. For my higher education, I pursued a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) at Lowry Memorial College, affiliated with Bangalore University. Later, I obtained a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) from Kamaraj University in Madurai, Tamil Nadu.

I have a strong passion for web development, and I work as a web developer. In my free time, I enjoy playing cricket, chess, and badminton.
